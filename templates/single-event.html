Our Mission

Equipping a generation for the final mission.

Generation of Youth for Christ (GYC) exists to inspire and equip young Adventists to pursue the fullness of the Holy Spirit, deepen their walk with God, and actively participate in fulfilling the gospel commission.

Every year, we gather thousands of young people from across the globe — united in purpose, stirred in spirit, and sent with urgency.

Worship & Revival

Spirit-filled praise, intercession, and powerful preaching that calls the heart back to Christ.

Discipleship

Seminars, workshops, and mentoring sessions designed to deepen faith and biblical literacy.

Mission & Service

Practical training and outreach opportunities to carry the gospel into your community and beyond.
